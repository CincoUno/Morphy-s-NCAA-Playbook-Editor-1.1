https://docs.google.com/document/d/1HkSXewOHgYPUl_6kk-8bEBma3RYwkzGtqDd5NIFmXy4/edit?usp=sharing

Use the tabs on the left of the Google Doc to sort through all the information. If you're looking to the answer of a specific problem, check the FAQ section or press CTRL + F and enter a key word related to your issue.

If you cannot find an answer there, go to the playbook discord, search the chat or troubleshooting channels by the same key word. You will most likely not be the first person with your issue.
